About Trinity International Public School

Holistic education for tomorrow’s global citizens.

Trinity International Public School (TIPS) in Lucknow is a co-educational English medium group of schools offering CBSE, ICSE and ISC curricula. With campuses at Amrapali Yojna and Eldeco Udyan-1 (Eldeco Colony / Bangla Bazar), TIPS focuses on a balanced blend of strong academics, sports, arts and life skills to ensure the all-round development of students.


Well-qualified teachers, modern classrooms and a caring environment help build confident, disciplined and responsible individuals who are ready to face the challenges of a rapidly changing world.


Vision: To provide every child with the tools, values and opportunities to learn, explore and discover their full potential.

Mission: To create learner-centred, safe and inclusive campuses where academic excellence goes hand-in-hand with character building and life-long learning.

Founder & Management

The vision and people behind Trinity International Public School.

Trinity International Public School was founded by Mr. B. L. Singh, Ex. Additional Commissioner, with a clear vision of creating a school where children learn to think independently, adapt to change and contribute positively to society. He laid the foundation of TIPS with the belief that education should focus on learning, relearning and active participation in the world around us.


Under his guidance, TIPS has grown from a single campus to a reputed group of schools in Lucknow, offering quality education through the CBSE, ICSE and ISC boards. His emphasis on discipline, values and modern infrastructure continues to shape the culture of the school.


The Amrapali Yojna (CBSE) campus is managed by B.S.S. Educational Centre, which looks after academic standards, staff development and overall administration. The Eldeco Udyan-1 campus follows the norms of the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations (CISCE) while upholding the same Trinity philosophy and ethos.
